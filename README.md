What This Is

An agentic content generation service for lawyer friends in Delhi. Given a trigger (news URL, legal topic, or draft text), the service runs 3 parallel flows that produce platform-ready legal commentary content — LinkedIn posts, Instagram Reels/YouTube Shorts, and long-form YouTube avatar videos.

**Legal constraint (non-negotiable):** All content is legal commentary and public awareness only — not legal advice. A hard disclaimer is injected into every LLM system prompt and appended to every piece of content. The disclaimer cannot be disabled.

> *"This content is legal commentary for public awareness only and does not constitute legal advice. For advice specific to your situation, consult a qualified lawyer."*

**Geography:** Delhi / India context baked into all prompts.
